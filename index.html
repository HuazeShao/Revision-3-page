Possible Human Extinction

What

Human extinction has always been the issue that studied by scientists throughout centuries. Along with the development of techlogy and population, more and more factors appeared to be considered as the causes of human extinction.

81M

Population is one of the greate causes that might lead to human extinction. The growth rate of population is averagely 1.05%. The average increase of population is 81 million per year. Such a great amount of population would cause supply crisis, which leads to malnourishment and death from hunger. The supply crsis would also lead to the violence -- facing limited resources, people might fight each other in order to gain the ownership of resources.

12%

Due to the development of science and technology, the amount of CO2 (Carbon dioxide) has been growing rapidly. According to researches, the global CO2 has increased 12% since year 2000. One great source of producing CO2 is fossile fuels. We consume large amount of fossil fules daily -- if we travel, heat up things, use electricity, then we are consuming fossil fuels.
